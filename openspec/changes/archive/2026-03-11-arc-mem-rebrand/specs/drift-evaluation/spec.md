## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Drift evaluation terminology update
Drift evaluation prompts and scoring descriptions SHALL use ARC-Mem terminology. References to "anchor" in evaluation prompts SHALL become "semantic unit". The epistemic hedging classification (NOT_MENTIONED, not CONTRADICTED) and composable scoring behavior remain unchanged.

#### Scenario: Drift prompts use ARC-Mem terms
- **WHEN** drift evaluation prompts reference the working-memory system
- **THEN** they SHALL use "semantic unit" and "activation score" terminology
